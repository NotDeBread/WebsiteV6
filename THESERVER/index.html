THE SERVER
'THE SERVER' is a modded Minecraft server originally made for my Computer Service Technology class?, but due to the lack of members, we opened it to the public. It has been given regular updates about once every month or so, adding content mods and fixing performance issues.

Active Players

Featured Builds
Below are some cool builds that have been made on THE SERVER. (Notice how almost all of them were made by Skywalkr)
The Citadel By Skywalkr
The Citadel is Skywalkr's second major base, being inside of a mountain near The Highlands. It has been under construction for about 3 months now.
The Tower By Skywalkr
The Tower was the first large build on the server, it being Skywalkr's first major base. It is now availble to rent out as no one lives there anymore.
The Highlands By DeBread
The Highlands was DeBread's second base, the one after The Pub & Grub. This build was originally going to be near a friends base, but ended up not happening.
New Bread City By Skywalkr, DeBread, Jag(?)
New Bread City is the 'Down Town' of THE SERVER. The first building there was the train station, made by DeBread. Then, Skywalkr designed and constructed the mall which is across the street. After those two, Skywalkr made the bank, shulker shell shop, post office, and Citadel Records.
Generations Village By Plonk, DeBread, Jaden, udev, Jakob, Roba, Wasp, Bees, Skywalkr
Generations Village was originally a 1.7 server hosted by Plonk. It was active for only a couple months until it was eventually ported into THE SERVER a few months ago via chunk blending.
